Description
Form Structure:

Step 1: Personal Information.
Step 2: Address Information.
Step 3: Review and Submit.
JavaScript Functions:

showStep(step): Controls the visibility of the steps.
nextStep(): Validates the current step and moves to the next.
prevStep(): Moves to the previous step.
validateCurrentStep(): Validates input data in the current step.
submitForm(): Submits the form after validating the final step.
Field Labels and Instructions:

Labels: Clearly associated with their respective input fields.
Error Messages: Displayed if validation fails.
Navigation and Validation:

Navigation: Users can move between steps and review information.
Validation: Ensures that each step is completed correctly before proceeding.
