Summary of Form Functionalities Included:
Mandatory Fields: Username is required and pre-populated with a default value.
Optional Fields: Phone number field is optional.
Field Format Constraints: Email field must contain an '@' symbol.
Input Masking: Password field must have at least 6 characters.
Conditional Fields: Address field appears based on a checkbox.
Field Limits: Age must be between 18 and 100.
Default Values: Event Date is pre-filled with a default value.
Auto-Save: Auto-saves the value of a field every 5 seconds.
CAPTCHA Simulation: Simple CAPTCHA check.
Form Reset and Submission: Handles reset and validation with error messages.
